Graphic design in the music industry

On this wedsite I will investigate the interesting relationship between graphic design and music, exploring how these two worlds connect to create a powerful impact.

I will examine iconic album covers, band logos and there meaning, innovative merchandise, concert and moving image visuals, and visual tactics used by artists to captivate their fans. on this wedsite I will take you on a journey to discover the main areas Graphic Design is used within the music industry. In my project book I will lead you through the following areas: · What is Graphic Design · The History and Origins of Graphic Design in the Music Industry · Album Cover Design · Band Logo Design · Design of Merchandise and Promotional Material · Graphic Design and Music Videos · Live Gigs and Graphic Design · Project Photo Timeline · Designing my album - Designing an Album Cover for a music artist Juju. So, join me on this visual journey to uncover the powerful fusion of graphic design and music.
